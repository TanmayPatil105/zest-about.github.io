COEP organises a National Level Inter-College Sports extravaganza called "ZEST" annually. ZEST’22 will consist of about 10 different sporting events.


To highlight the unavoidable footing of technology in sport and to shade the future of sports among the youth, this year we have come up with the theme "Futuristic Metanoia", through which we aim to the embed importance of technology in sports culture. Ability of evolution and adaptation is necessary for the survival. As from the Ancient time,Sports is adapting the new things which are related to development and inventions in Technology and other many factors. Sports is a very important part of our life and it is accepting the new progress. It will improve the version, but it won’t become history. In the Future, Everything will change, however Sports will be the constant part of life.
